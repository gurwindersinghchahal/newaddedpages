Our Services

Self Managed Super Funds Audit

The audit of Self Managed Superannuation Funds (SMSF) is one of our specialised areas. Being ASIC Registered SMSF Auditor, our audit team will perform the financial and compliance audit for the SMSF.

Trust Accounts Audit

Trust accounts audits are compliance audits of Trust Accounts. We conduct Trust Account Audits in accordance with Australian Auditing Standards.

Incorporated Association Audits

Our assurance team assist Queensland based Incorporated Associations meet their audit requirements under the Associations Incorporation Act 1981.

Body Corporate Audits

Working with third party Body Corporate managers and current Body Corporate committee’s , we will ensure that independence is maintained from the Body Corporate management and the Committee to maintain the objective nature of any assessment given
