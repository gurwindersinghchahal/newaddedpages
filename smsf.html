Self Managed Superannuation Funds Audits

The audit of Self Managed Superannuation Funds (SMSF) is one of our specialised areas. Being ASIC Registered SMSF Auditor, our audit team will perform the financial and compliance audit for the SMSF under the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act 1993 (SISA) and Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Regulations 1994 (SISR). We will report on the financial and compliance status of the SMSF through our professional audit report.
